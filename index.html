--- layout: single title: "Home" permalink: / author_profile: true --- I'm a journalist at the Reuters Institute for the Study of Journalism. Our work focuses on covering journalism, the media industry, and the public that they serve. I am particularly interested in issues surrounding labour, disinformation, AI, and the Global South. I am hoping to experiment more with data and visualizations. I previously was a journalist with the Canadian Broadcasting Corporation (CBC) in Montreal, Québec where I covered daily local and breaking news for radio (and sometimes web). I hold a Bachelor's degree in Honours International Development from McGill University and a Master's degree in Comparative Social Policy from the University of Oxford.
